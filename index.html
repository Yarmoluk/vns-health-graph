Frictions #4 & #5
The cascades that compound silently

The Varis Trap

Varis requests records. Provider has 30 days. Miss it? Full claim reclamation. Provider did the work. Patient got the care. Missed admin deadline = zero pay.

After this happens twice, provider stops accepting VNS members. VNS sees "network attrition" and doesn't connect it to Varis.

Graph fix: Provider risk scoring. Flag 2+ recoupments + declining claims + no portal logins. Proactive VNS outreach 15 days before Varis deadline. One call saves the relationship.

Knowledge Walking Out the Door

130 years of knowledge lives in people. Which providers return calls. Which aides are reliable where. Which members need their daughter called first.

When veteran staff retire — especially multilingual staff in a 50+ language market — the entire relationship network of a care territory vanishes.

Graph fix: Institutional knowledge capture. On retirement announcement → generate full profile: relationships, operational insights, community knowledge → transfer to successor.

The Connection

Varis erodes providers → breaks member-provider relationships → drops CAHPS → hits star ratings → reduces enrollment revenue → constrains hiring → accelerates knowledge drain. It's one system. The graph shows the whole system.
